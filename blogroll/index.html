Who should I read?

An important question for any new Salesforce Admin! I asked the Power of Us HUB what they read, and my list got a LOT longer. Here's a list of blogs that are helpful to non-profit SF admins.

  • ButtonClickAdmin -- A necessary blog and podcast for any SF admin.
  • AdminHero -- Another MUST for getting and staying up to speed as an admin.
  • ClicksNotCode -- Some helpful tips for declarative development.
  • Developing Flow -- A new tutorial and blog all about Visual Workflow.
  • Melissa VanDyke -- A newish blog vor the ambitious admin.
  • KELL Labs -- An active blog from a very good non-profit partner.
  • SalesforceYoda -- High quality content on Flow and other point and click life.
  • The Wizard's News -- Very good blog posts and youtube training videos about SF!
  • Cloud4Good -- Another awesome and active partner blog focusing on non-profit issues.
  • BrightStepPartners -- A partner who can help, and lots of good content on WHY.
  • The Salesforce Dude -- An up and coming blog on how to be a great admin.
  • Women Code Heroes -- High quality and very accessible content on Developer and Admin issues.
  • Mary Scotton -- A great SF blogger, designer, and hero.
